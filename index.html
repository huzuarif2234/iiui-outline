Cybersecurity Training Outline

Title: The Role of Cybersecurity in Today’s Digital World: Applications and Cybersecurity Roadmap

Total Duration: 2 Hours (60 min Theory + 60 min Practical)

Part 1: Theory (60 min)

Understanding Cybersecurity Concepts & Roadmap

  • Welcome and Overview (5 min) – Purpose of cybersecurity
  • Cybersecurity Basics (10 min) – CIA Triad, domains of security
  • Social Engineering Concepts (10 min) – Types and psychology
  • Case Studies (10 min) – Twitter hack, Kevin Mitnick
  • Career Roadmap (15 min) – Roles, skills, certifications
  • Emerging Trends (5 min) – AI, Zero Trust, Quantum crypto
  • Q&A and Recap (5 min)

Part 2: Practical (60 min)

Social Engineering Tools and Demonstrations

  • Ethical Use Disclaimer (5 min) – Safety and legality
  • Zphisher Demo (15 min)
    • Generate phishing page
    • Collect credentials via ngrok
    • Defense: URL checking, 2FA
  • Grabify/IPLogger (10 min)
    • Track IP and device info
    • Defense: Avoid unknown links, use VPN
  • Telegram Bot (10 min)
    • Setup BotFather
    • Send data to Telegram using script
  • Hardware Attack (15 min)
    • Digispark/Arduino script to launch payload
    • Simulate phishing via USB injection
    • Defense: Disable USB autorun
  • Wrap-up and Discussion (5 min) – Ethical hacking, tools to explore

Bonus Resources

  • Learning Platforms: TryHackMe, Hack The Box, OverTheWire
  • PDF Handout (Optional): Roadmap + Tools + Hygiene Checklist
  • Certifications: Security+, CEH, OSCP, CISSP
